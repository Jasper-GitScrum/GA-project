2. Which of these is a key feature of Agile Project Management?
Bravo! You got it correct! Incorrect. The right answer is B) Flexibility and incremental progress. Agile Project Management is characterized by its adaptability to changing requirements and focuses on continuous improvement, customer feedback, and delivering essential components in short cycles. This approach contrasts with traditional methods that involve extensive planning and rigid project scopes. Understanding Agile's emphasis on flexibility and iterative progress is crucial for modern project management practices.
3. What does the term 'Scope Creep' refer to in project management?
Bravo! You got it correct! Incorrect. The correct answer is A) The increase in project scope without adjustments to time, cost, and resources. Scope Creep occurs when new elements are added to a project without corresponding increases in resources, time, or budget, leading to project overruns and challenges in successful delivery. It often results from poor project definition, lack of proper initial identification of what is required to bring about the project objectives, or changes in project scope without adjustment to time and cost. Managing scope effectively is critical in project management to ensure project success.
